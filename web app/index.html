FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What can we help you with?

  • Do I need any deep learning expertise to use this app?

    No. You just have to click on "Orchids" in the "select a category" section and upload an picture.

  • How I do get started with the app?

    Click on "Orchids" in the "Select a category" section and upload an image. It will show details of the flower.

  • Will the image resolution affect the quality of recognition?

    This application works across a wide range of image resolution. For best results, we recommend using 680*480px or higher. Going below 320*480px, may increase the chances of missing details or inappropriate recognitions.

  • What image formats does it supports?

    Currently, it supports JPG, JPEG and PNG image formats.
